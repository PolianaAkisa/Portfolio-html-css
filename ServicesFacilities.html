    HEALTH SERVICES

    Sancare preparatory school attends to the health issues of every student by taking them to a clinic that is managed by a qualified nurse. The clinic attends to minor illnesses and first aid services.

    Some of the health services provided in the clinic include:

    1. Health Education, Personal hygiene, Healthy leaving and the transition into adolescence.

    2. Mentoring & Counselling

    3. First Aid Training

    4. Safety;The importance of safe play and focus are regularly emphasized.

    CATERING SERVICES

    Our Catering Department offers a wide range of nutritious, well balanced and delicious meals that meet the needs and tastes of students from pre-school to primary level. We also cater to the needs of students on special diets. Each school has a team of teachers who supervise meal times to monitor service delivery and student satisfaction.
